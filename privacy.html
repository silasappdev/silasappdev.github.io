Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2025

Rhyno App Studios built the CDL Study App as a free app with optional premium features. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our app. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being fully transparent about our data practices.

Information We Collect

CDL Study App stores the following information locally on your device only. This data is never transmitted to our servers or any third party:

  • Study time and session data
  • Quiz scores and progress
  • Flagged questions and weak areas
  • Your selected CDL class preference
  • Number of quizzes and questions completed

This information exists solely on your device to provide you with progress tracking features. If you delete the app all of this data is permanently removed.

Information We Do Not Collect

We do not collect any of the following:

  • Your name or personal identity
  • Email address or phone number
  • Location data of any kind
  • Contacts or address book data
  • Photos videos or audio
  • Browsing or search history
  • Financial or payment information
  • Health or fitness data
  • Any sensitive personal information

We do not require you to create an account to use CDL Study App.

How We Use Your Information

The data stored locally on your device is used exclusively to:

  • Display your study time and progress on the home screen
  • Track which questions need more practice
  • Remember your CDL class preference between sessions
  • Show your quiz history and average scores

Third Party Services

CDL Study App does not currently use any third party analytics advertising or tracking services. All data is stored locally on your device only.

Future versions of the app may include advertising services and optional premium subscription features. This privacy policy will be updated to reflect any changes before they take effect. When advertising is added it may involve collection of device identifiers for ad serving purposes and users will be notified.

In App Purchases

Premium subscriptions are processed entirely by Apple through the App Store. We do not collect store or have access to any payment information. Please refer to Apple's Privacy Policy at apple.com/privacy for information about how Apple handles payment data.

Data Security

Since all data is stored locally on your device the security of your information depends on your device security settings. We recommend using a device passcode or biometric authentication to protect your device and the data stored on it.

Data Retention

Your data is retained on your device until you delete the app or clear the app's data through your device settings. We have no ability to access modify or delete data stored on your device.

Children's Privacy

CDL Study App is intended for users who are 18 years of age or older as CDL licensing requires drivers to be at least 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has used our app please contact us and we will take appropriate steps.

Your Rights

Since we do not collect personal data on our servers there is no personal data for us to provide access to modify or delete. All data is stored on your device and can be removed by deleting the app.

Changes To This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time especially as we add new features like advertising or premium subscriptions. We will notify you of any significant changes by updating the date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this policy periodically. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
